The Best Movies According to Sindhuja

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kadeisi Vivasayi

The story is about an octogenarian farmer's passion for agriculture and his kindness towards living beings. However, he was falsely accused of killing peacocks, which delayed his cultivation as he was the last farmer in the village to provide the first grain for the village Temple ceremony. Finally, he was proven innocent and set free. The temple rituals began with his cultivated paddy crops.

Taare Zameen Par

Ishaan is criticised by his parents for his poor academic performance and is sent away to a boarding school. Ram, an art teacher, however, realises he has dyslexia and helps him uncover his potential.

Manjummel boys

After one of them falls inside a dangerous pit in the Guna Caves, a group of childhood friends muster courage to rescue him out of a space from where a return is next to impossible.
